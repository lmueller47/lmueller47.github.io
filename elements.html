About Me

Why do I make games?
The answer is really simple. I just love it. I enjoy nearly every aspect of it from backend programming to art and animation. If you're ever wondering what I'm doing, it's probably something to do with game design.

I have been programming games since 2017, learning the ins and outs of Unity, Blender, Visual Studio, Photoshop, Illustrator, and other helpful tools for game developement. Over the course of my journey I've used many programming languages including Javascript, C#, Python, and more. I have made numerous projects since I first taught myself Unity that have allowed me to develop and fine tune the vital skills needed to succeed in this competitive industry.

I am ready to continue my journey and to pursue my passions with a team that shares my love for everything art, design, and programming.
