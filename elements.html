About Me

I am an artist who loves to use the medium of games.
I have a passion for creating worlds and experiences that leave an impact on those who play my games. I believe art has been and always be the best way to communicate and connect with people without ever saying a word.

I have been designing games since 2017, learning the ins and outs of Unity, Blender, Visual Studio, Photoshop, Illustrator, and other helpful tools for game developement. I have made numerous projects since I first taught myself Unity that have allowed me to develop and fine tune the vital skills like concepting, modeling, shading, animation, and many more needed to succeed in game design.

I am ready to continue my journey and to pursue my passions with a team that shares my love for everything game art and design.
