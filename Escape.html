Escape Room Marshfield Puzzles

This project was contracted to me by Escape Room Marshfield in the Summer of 2023. The project consisted of making a number of digital puzzles to be used in their next escape room. One of the unique challenges of the project was developing a physical interface for players to interact with while solving the puzzles. I made use of the makey-makey board to map buttons to computer inputs like the arrow keys and mouse buttons. Using this strategy I can place a button on each wall of the room, forcing one player to be on each button, the players are forced to communicate with each other to solve the puzzles. This adds a new fun and interesting level of complexity to seemingly easy puzzles. Designing this game helped me refine not only programming skills, but helped to reinforce my design philosophy of focusing on designing for people rather than designing the next, most amazing game mechanics.

I recently was able to display the project at the MAGD expo in 2024 for the project recieved an honorable mention for best in show for Real World Application. I will continue to refine this project in my free time until its upcoming release. The room is expected to open before the start of next school year around August of 2024.
