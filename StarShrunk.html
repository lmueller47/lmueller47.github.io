StarShrunk

This game was developed for the Global Game Jam 2023. The theme for the jam was roots, so the game was designed around square rooting the player's size thus the character shrink to increasingly smaller environments. For this project I took the art lead and worked with a team of three artists to develop the art for the game. My main focus was environment design as well as most of the character designs for the enemies and the final one-eyed boss. I also had to manage my team of artist and cooridnate them to follow an art bible to ensure the style of the game remained similar for each individual artist.

For this project I went a little out of my comfort zone and worked with sound design. I worked in a recording studio to produce some sound effects for the game. I also worked with the programmers of the project to help troubleshoot as well as to help with the development of the gravity system. The programming team was fairly new, but I got the chance to share my experience with them and help them through the process of coding for video games.
